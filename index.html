SPACED

A gritty top-down sci-fi party RPG. Survival, ship life, tactical combat, dialogue choices, stealth, crime, and a galaxy worth of bad decisions.

Quick controls

PC & Mobile: Tap or click any tile or entity to see available actions — move, talk, loot, or interact. You must be within 5 tiles to talk or loot. Use the ▼ button to collapse the bottom bar on small screens.

Zoom & pan: Scroll wheel to zoom. Pinch on touch. Drag empty map space to pan. Use + / − buttons in the top bar.

Combat: Click enemies to attack. Use ability buttons in the bottom bar. Press End Turn when done.
