STARFALL DIRECTIVE

A gritty top-down sci-fi party RPG with survival, ship life, tactical combat, dialogue choices, stealth, crime, and galaxy travel.

Starter controls

PC: left click to move/interact/select, right click on entities/items for quick actions, middle mouse or hold space + drag to pan camera, wheel to zoom.

Phone: tap to move/interact/select, long press for quick actions, drag empty map space to pan, pinch or buttons to zoom.

Combat: same map as exploration. Use action buttons, movement grid, inventories, and abilities.
